About me

I am an AI research engineer and a PhD student currently pursuing a degree in Software Engineering and Artificial Intelligence.

A few reasons why I decided to start this blog:
• I want to improve and deepen my knowledge by explaining CS concepts to others.
• I want to give back to the community, and I hope that somebody will find this useful.

Prior to entering the world of computer science, I dreamed of becoming a basketball player. At 15 years old, I had the chance to leave my home in Serbia and move to distant Spain, where I played for Real Madrid. After a year, I returned to Serbia and played for Red Star. My next stop was Los Angeles, USA, where I received a full scholarship at LMU, a Division I school, where I was part of the basketball team and an applied math major. This was an amazing journey, but it eventually came to an end. Today, I try to bring the same hardworking mindset and competitive spirit I developed in sports to computer science. I hope that through this blog, I will get a chance to meet new people who share the same passions as I do.

I will not use AI to generate any of the content I post here. I want this content to be authentic and raw.
